Frequently asked questions

How does RapidReply work?▼
When a customer calls your business and you miss the call, RapidReply automatically sends them a text within 60 seconds with a numbered menu (schedule service, emergency repair, or get a quote). When they reply, the system routes their request and alerts you with their name and number instantly.
Will this work with my current phone number?▼
Yes. We set up a dedicated business number that works alongside your existing one. Your customers call the same number, and the system handles the missed-call detection and texting behind the scenes. We take care of all the technical setup for you.
Do I need to install any apps or software?▼
No. RapidReply runs entirely in the background. You don't need to download anything, log into a dashboard, or change how you run your business. Leads and alerts come to you via text message — the same way your customers reach you.
What do the automated texts say?▼
The initial text apologizes for the missed call and gives the customer three options: schedule service, request emergency repair, or get a quote. The messages are friendly and professional — you can watch the full conversation flow in the interactive demo above.
Is this a chatbot or AI?▼
No. RapidReply uses pre-written text templates and a numbered menu system. The customer picks 1, 2, or 3, and the system routes them accordingly. It's simple, reliable, and doesn't try to have a conversation — it just connects the lead to you as fast as possible.
Is this compliant with texting regulations?▼
Yes. The customer initiates contact by calling your business, which provides implied consent for a response. All messages include opt-out language ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe"), and we handle A2P 10DLC compliance registration as part of your setup.
How long does setup take?▼
We can have you up and running within an hour. Setup is done for you — we configure the number, the workflows, and the alert routing. You just tell us your business name and we handle the rest.
