FAQ

What if I get 100+ calls a day? ▼
RapidReply scales with you. Whether you get 10 or 1,000 calls daily, our system handles it instantly. You pay per conversation, so costs scale too.
Will customers actually reply? ▼
Yes. Our data shows 40%+ reply rates from auto-SMS sent within 60 seconds. That's dramatically higher than waiting until the next business day.
Can I customize the message? ▼
Absolutely. You control the template. We handle the timing and delivery. You stay in control of your brand voice.
What if someone calls from out of state? ▼
RapidReply works nationwide. Whether your customer is in Fort Bend County or California, you'll still capture that lead in 60 seconds.
How does the AI work? ▼
Our AI analyzes call metadata (time, caller info, repeat caller status) and crafts contextual responses. You get smarter lead qualification without any extra work.
